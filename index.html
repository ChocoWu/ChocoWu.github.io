Shengqiong Wu (吴胜琼)

I am currently a M.S. student advised by Prof. Donghong Ji in School of Cyber Science and Engineering at Wuhan University, after I finished my undergraduate study at the School of Computer Science , Wuhan University.

I am currently working on Opinion Mining and Sentiment Analysis, and partially Syntax Parsing. I have employed the external syntactic and linguistic features to aid aforementioned NLP tasks. In my future research, I would continue the exploration of syntax-aided NLP, such as implicit syntax modeling, syntax-semantics co-parsing, and syntax-enhanced multi-modal representation learning, etc.
